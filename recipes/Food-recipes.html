Pizza

Sausage and Alfredo pizza. So good you won't believe just how easy it is to make! It's kind of messy to eat, but totally worth it

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • ½ cup prepared Alfredo sauce
  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Italian cheese blend

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Crumble the sausage into a skillet over medium-high heat.
    Cook until evenly browned. Add onion, and cook for a few minutes.
    Drain excess grease, and stir in the Alfredo sauce.
    Spoon this mixture over the pre-baked pizza crust.
    Sprinkle cheese over the top.
  3. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cheese is melted.
    Allow to cool slightly before cutting to allow the toppings to set.

Sweet Breakfast Sandwich

Super easy and delicious sandwich to make for breakfast or a snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)
  • 2 English muffins, split and toasted
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons marshmallow cream

Steps

  1. Spread 1 tablespoon chocolate-hazelnut spread on 1 half of each English muffin
  2. arrange banana slices on top. Spread 1 tablespoon marshmallow cream on the other half
  3. Press both halves together to create a sandwich.

Chicken Burgers

A tasty chicken burger that has spices added to it to make a good burger to eat with a salad, or you could add it to soups or casseroles to make them stretch further.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grain bread, torn into chunks
  • ½ cup quick-cooking oats
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin,1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 onion, finely chopped ,1 egg And 2 tablespoons canola oil

Steps

  1. Process bread in a food processor until you have fine bread crumbs.
  2. Stir bread crumbs, oats, salt, mustard, celery seed, and cumin together in a bowl. Mix chicken, onion, and egg together in a separate bowl; add bread crumb mixture to chicken mixture and mix. Divide and shape mixture into 7 patties.
  3. Press both halves together to create a sandwich.
  4. Heat canola oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook patties in hot oil until browned each side and no longer pink the center, about 5 minutes per side.
